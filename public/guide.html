User Guide

Everything you need to know about using HenHud Helps

How to Submit an Item

  1. Click "Submit Item" from the navigation bar on the top of your screen or in the main hompage at the center.
  2. Fill in the details of the item. (Photos are optional)
  3. Click submit to publish the missing or found item. Admin review and approval of your submission is required prior to posting.

When Item is Found

  1. To idenitfy if the item has been found go to the catalog section and look at the status bar on the item.
  2. Claim your item: Pick up your lost item in the front office or as noted in the additional comments provided.

Contact Support

If you have any questions, contact the HenHud Helps Team
