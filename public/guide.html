User Guide

Everything you need to know about using HenHud Helps

How to Submit an Item

  1. Login is required to submit a lost or found item.
  2. Click "Submit Item" from the navigation bar at the top of your screen or in the main homepage at the center.
  3. Fill in the details of the item. (Photos are optional)
  4. Click submit to publish the missing or found item.

When Item is Found

  1. To identify if the item has been labeled missing, found, or returned, go to the catalog section and look at the status bar on the item.
  2. Look at the information of the item, checking the item name, date and time, location, and any additional comments in the section.
  3. Claim your item: Pick up the item in the location last seen or as noted in the additional comments provided.

Contact Support+Additional Information

  1. If you have any questions contact the Hen Hud Helps support group members via email
