User Guide

Everything you need to know about using HenHud Helps

How to Submit an Item

  1. Click "Submit Item" from the navigation bar on the top of your screen or in the main hompage at the center.
  2. Fill in the details of the item. (Photos are optional)
  3. Click submit to publish the missing or found item.

When Item is Found

  1. To idenitfy if the item has been labled missing or found go to the catalog section and look at the status bar on the item.
  2. Look at the information of the item checking the item name, date and time, location, and any additoional comments in the section.
  3. Pick up the item in the location last seen.

Contact Support+Additional Information

  1. If there are any questions contact our support group members which is etramso@students.henhudschools.org and jucrockettlionnet@students.henhudschools.org
  2. At the top of the navigation section there is a person icon DO NOT click on it unless you are authorized to be an admin ONLY admins will be able to log in.
